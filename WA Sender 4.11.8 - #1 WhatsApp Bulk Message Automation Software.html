Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about WA Sender

Is WA Sender safe to use? Will my WhatsApp account get banned?

WA Sender is designed with safety in mind. Our smart delay system, batch sending, and gradual volume increase features help protect your account. We recommend following WhatsApp's guidelines and avoiding spam messages.

What's the difference between the plans?

All plans include the full WA Sender software with unlimited features. The Personal plan is for individual use. The Reseller plan adds an unlimited online panel to generate licenses for your clients. The Distributor plan lets you create multiple resellers, each with their own panel.

Do I need to save contacts before sending messages?

No! WA Sender allows you to send messages to unsaved numbers directly. Simply import your contact list from CSV/Excel or paste the numbers, and you're ready to go.

Is this a one-time payment?

Yes! All our plans are lifetime licenses with one-time payment. No monthly fees, no hidden costs. You pay once and use forever, including all future updates.

Can I send media files with messages?

Yes! WA Sender supports sending images, videos, documents, and audio files. You can attach media to make your messages more engaging and informative.
